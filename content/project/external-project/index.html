Breaking down the data to the level of cases per county over the last 2 weeks allows us to observe the current infection hotspots nationwide in a manner which is obscured when viewing the total amount of cases per county.





Counties most affected

As we can see in the map, Dublin and Cavan have suffered the highest relative proportion of cases, followed by Monaghan, Westmeath, Kildare and Wicklow. As of 7-Aug , it is mainly counties of the west that have been least affected
